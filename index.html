About Sonar

Introducing Sonar, a personalized well-being companion for young people and their families. 80% of youth who show signs of mental illness do not receive any support or treatment (CDC, 2023). Sonar addresses this issue by

  1. Noticing moments for support based on analysis of online and mobile activity (with consent)
  2. Offering personalized notifications and recommendations
  3. Providing an outlet for youth to request support or seek anonymous guidance
